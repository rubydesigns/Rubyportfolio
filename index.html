iCloud Private Relay Mode in Safari

Lead Designer • Google Search Trust Team

The Problem

When iCloud Private Relay is turned on in iOS settings, it obscures a user's IP address. For Safari users on Google.com that have this setting turned on, this means location-dependent searches often returned results with an inaccurate location.

The Results

I designed a callout card for users with obscured IP addresses to inform them of how to adjust their location preferences and correct location errors.

  • Boosted "Use precise location" CTR from 0.84% to 2.3%.
  • Anticipating increase of users providing precise location.
